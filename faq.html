🤔 Need help?
Find answers to common questions
🤔 How to save a tip? ▼
Click the "🔖 Save Tip" button on any tip detail page to add to your saved items. You can view saved items in the Saved Items page.
📍 How to find shops near me? ▼
Go to Shops page and tap "Get My Location". Allow location access when prompted. You can also search by area name like "Ipoh", "Cheras", or "Subang".
💬 How to post in community? ▼
Go to Community page and tap "Create New Post". Fill in your title and content, then publish. Your post will appear in the community feed.
💡 Can I share my own tips? ▼
Yes! Go to Profile page and fill the "Share a tip" form. Enter title, description, and your name. Your tip will appear on Home page and All Tips page.
🔍 Why no shops found? ▼
Try increasing the search radius to 5km or 10km. Some areas may have limited data. You can also try searching a different area like a nearby city.
🌙 How to enable Dark Mode? ▼
Go to Settings page and toggle the Dark Mode switch. The whole app will switch to dark theme. Your preference is saved automatically.
🔖 Where are my saved tips? ▼
Tap "Saved" in the quick links section on Home page or Profile page. All your bookmarked tips will appear there.
